07 / My Growth Story

Reflection & Self-Assessment

📍 Where I Started

"At the beginning of this module, I had limited understanding of professional communication and business-related skills in IT. I was primarily focused on technical content and had not deeply considered the role communication plays in a technology career."

⚠️ Challenges I Faced

  • Public speaking anxiety during presentations
  • Limited confidence when engaging with clients or peers professionally
  • Bridging the gap between technical knowledge and communicating it clearly
  • Managing time between coursework and personal certification study

🧩 Technical Growth

Through my coursework and CompTIA Network+ preparation, I developed a much more solid understanding of networking fundamentals. My research paper on cybersecurity also introduced me to real-world threat models and risk management concepts that I had only read about abstractly before.

🗣️ Communication Growth

My Semester 2 presentation was noticeably more confident and better structured than my first. The CRM role play and interview simulation pushed me well outside my comfort zone — but they were the most valuable experiences because they directly prepared me for professional environments.

💡 Key Lessons Learned

  • Communication is as critical as technical skill in IT careers
  • IT is not only about code — it involves people, systems, and professional relationships
  • Continuous learning is not optional in this field; it is the baseline
  • Self-awareness about weaknesses is a strength, not a vulnerability

🚀 Future Application

"I will use the communication and professional skills gained in this module alongside my technical certifications to present myself confidently in professional environments. My goal is to enter the cybersecurity field with both the technical and human skills necessary to succeed."

📋 My Self-Improvement Plan

  • Complete CompTIA Security+ and Linux+ certifications
  • Gain hands-on networking experience through home labs and simulations
  • Practise professional communication regularly through presentations and writing
  • Build a stronger portfolio of projects that demonstrate real-world application
  • Stay current with cybersecurity news and emerging threats
